    Grading Policy

    For all CS classes, the grade breakdown is:

    Component Percentage
    Assessments: Exams and Quizzes 50% of the final grade.
    Labs 30% of the final grade.
    Classwork + Participation 20% of the final grade.

    Dropped Lowest Assessments

    I do my best to drop the lowest grade if possible. The exceptions would be any heavily weighted assessment such as a midterm / final exam / final project.

    I only reflect this drop in the final grade for the course, not in the first or second marking periods.

    Missed Exams

    There is a schoolwide policy for missing any quiz/test without a valid excuse. I will be following that.

    There will be an email from the school that outlines this policy.

    The only addendum to this policy is as follows:

    Any assignment penalized for unexcused absence or academic dishonesty will NOT be dropped no matter the score.

    Late Work

    I want to encourage: hand it in on time, don't try to work on it for a week after it is due.

    Late work including labs and projects will be penalized 10% per school day that the assignment is late. The maximum penalty is 50%, however late work will not be accepted after the marking period it is due.

    You each get 5 late tokens, good for 1 day late for 1 assignment* to avoid this penalty.

    You must email me to use your late tokens, I am not refreshing your work to see when you submit things, and google classroom comments do not alert me.

    *Any assignment except the final project. The intent is not that you can save tokens to get extra time on the final project.
